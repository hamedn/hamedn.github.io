Hamed Nilforoshan

I am the CEO and Co-Founder of HiringCafe. Our mission is to help every individual achieve economic mobility. We are building a real-time dataset of every job on earth, and the AI to match every individual to the perfect job.

Previously, I was a Computer Science PhD student at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Jure Leskovec. Before that, I was a Machine Learning Data Scientist at Airbnb.

I work at the intersection of AI and terabyte-scale, messy, noisy data. During my PhD, I developed data science and AI methods for healthcare and public policy ( Highlights: Nature '23, ICML '22 Best Paper Award). I am grateful to have been supported by the Knight-Hennessy and NSF Graduate Research Fellowships.

Before starting my PhD, I worked as a Machine Learning Data Scientist at Airbnb. I designed several algorithms, currently in production, to mitigate gun violence, money-laundering, and credit card theft on the platform.

Prior to Airbnb, I worked at Snapchat, where I built SliceNDice, a graph-based method used to catch botnets and rings of fraudulent advertisers. Two years later, SliceNDice is still in production, and has resulted in two patents in addition to the publication.
